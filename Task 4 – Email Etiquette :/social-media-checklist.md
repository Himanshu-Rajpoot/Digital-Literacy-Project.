# Social Media Do's and Don'ts – Exclusive College Student Edition

## Do's

* Do pause and reflect before posting: ensure your content aligns with your personal and professional values.
* Do verify sources and information to avoid spreading false content.
* Do maintain digital boundaries: separate personal and academic/professional accounts.
* Do engage respectfully, giving constructive feedback rather than negative criticism.
* Do regularly review privacy settings and remove outdated or potentially harmful posts.

## Don'ts

* Don't share anything online that you wouldn’t be comfortable with a professor, employer, or family member seeing.
* Don't respond impulsively to conflicts or criticisms; avoid heated online arguments.
* Don't post sensitive or confidential academic material without permission.
* Don't participate in trending challenges or memes that could harm your reputation.
* Don't overexpose your personal life or location, which could compromise safety or privacy.

## Purpose

This checklist helps college students maintain a professional, safe, and responsible online presence while avoiding common pitfalls that can affect reputation, academics, and career opportunities.
